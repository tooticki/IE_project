EQUATIONS WITH RIGHT HAND SIDE MEASURE
MICHELE COLTURATO AND MARCO DEGIOVANNI
Abstract. We consider a quasilinear elliptic equation, with right hand side
measure, which does not satisfy the usual coercivity assumption. We prove an
existence result in the line of the Fredholm alternative. For this purpose we
develop a variant of degree theory suited to this setting.
